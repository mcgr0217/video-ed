How do i Cancel?
  • Go to the account tab and click "Cancel my subscription." It's that simple.
How do I export at 1080p?
  • Pro accounts have access to free 1080p exports. If you have a premium account, you can purchase individual 1080p exports. Free users do not have access to 1080p exports.
Can I get more capacity?
  • Go to the account tab and click "Cancel my subscription." It's that simple.
What happens at the end of my subscription? How do I renew?
  • Your subscriptions will auto-renew at the end of each term. The auto-renewal will be for the same duration as your original subscription. You can cancel your subscription at any time.
How do I download my created video?
  • You can easily download your video by clicking the green Download button on the video page.
How is WeVideo for Education different from a Premium account?
  • Education accounts can invite multiple students to upload media, edit videos and publish. Students can also share media and collaborate on editing projects. Premium accounts only allow one user.
  • Education accounts allow admins to control student permissions, student export approvals, and track user activity (reporting). Teachers can also choose to allow or disallow social sharing. Premium account have none of these capabilities.
